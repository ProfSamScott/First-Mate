What is This?

This app uses Monte Carlo Simulation to help you predict where your opponents' ships will be when you play a BattleShip game with the same rules as BattleFriends at Sea.

You can load this up on your tablet while you play and flip back and forth to get predictions. Even if you restart your browser, the assistant will remember the games you had in process.

The assistant assumes two ships each of lengths 4, 3, and 2, and it assumes that your opponents are not allowed to place their ships adjacent to one another. Because of the use of Monte Carlo Simulation, the assistant will work best against players who place their ships randomly.

What Do I Do?

  • Tap the "new" icon to create a new opponent.
  • Tap locations on the board to enter hits (pink *), misses (blue M), and ships you have found (orange S)
  • Press the compute button and the assistant will show you (in red) where you should fire your next shot
  • The numbers that appear tell you the percentage chance that there is a ship in that spot
  • IMPORTANT: Once you have finished killing a ship, you must change the * symbols to S symbols

What Else Do I Do?

  • Use the arrows to move between saved opponents
  • Use the delete button to remove an opponent
  • Use the clear button to keep the opponent but clear the board for a new game
